Six API Endpoints. Complete Provenance.

Drop-in REST API. One line of code. Full EU AI Act compliance.

POST/classify

Classify content as HUMAN, MACHINE, or INDETERMINATE. Uses statistical analysis: compression ratio, entropy, n-gram deviation, sentence structure variance.

POST/gate

Gate your training pipeline. Send data in, get back an admit/reject decision with provenance certificate. Blocks contaminated data before it reaches your model.

POST/decay

Predict quality at any generation depth. Returns the full McCaffer-Bovill decay curve and ncollapse — the exact point where your model becomes unsafe.

POST/watermark

Embed invisible provenance markers in AI-generated content. Survives copy-paste. Machine-recoverable. Proves content origin for downstream consumers.

POST/verify

Verify content provenance. Extracts watermarks, classifies origin, estimates generational depth, and predicts remaining fidelity.

POST/certify

Issue a cryptographically signed provenance certificate. HMAC-SHA256 sealed. Tamper-evident. Legally defensible audit trail for regulators.
